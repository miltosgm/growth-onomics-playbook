The 6-Step Implementation Roadmap

1

Feed Lead Sources

Connect Apollo, LinkedIn, or CSV exports to Google Sheets. Set up your "Raw Leads" input.

2

Enrich Automatically

Add Zapier workflows to clean data and call enrichment APIs (Apollo, Clearbit).

3

Score Against ICP

Build scoring logic: industry match, company size, role, geography. High scorers get top priority.

4

Generate Outreach

Use ChatGPT/Claude to write personalized first-touch emails. Store drafts as CRM notes.

5

Push to CRM

Create or update contacts in HubSpot/Pipedrive. Attach ICP score and email draft.

6

Daily Summaries

Pull yesterday's results. Send team a summary: leads processed, strong-fit count, top 3 names.
